Our Story

Built on Honesty and Hard Work

The Honest Mechanic is Adrian, a mobile mechanic serving Kyle and the surrounding areas. He started this with a simple idea: give people a mechanic they can actually trust, without the shop markup.

People find good mechanics hard to come by, so we built our whole reputation on being the opposite. Straight answers, fair labor pricing, and showing up when we say we will. That is why we hold a 100 percent recommendation rating.

Whether your car will not start in a parking lot or you need a second opinion before you buy, we come to you and tell it like it is.
